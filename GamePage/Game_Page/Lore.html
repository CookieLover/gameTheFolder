Lore

Castle:

Where the Prince used to hold prisoners captive. He abbandoned it ten years before present day when he became King and had power to change the justice system. The Prince held these prisoners because he believed that the justice system was inaffective and sloppy, so he used the castle to give the criminals the punishment he believed they deserved.


Fusei, the white ninja:

When his brother was studying in the Kingdom, he was framed for commiting a crime. Murder. Thankfully, the court decided to dismiss him, but the Prince abducted him and held him captive in a castle where he was tortured to death. Since he recieved the news, Fusei made it his mission to torture the Prince to death.


Yusha, the player:

Fusei's best friend. Since Fusei made it his mission to get revenge, Yusha has been trying to talk him out of it, but it hasn't worked. Now that Fusei has taken action, Yusha must, too.
